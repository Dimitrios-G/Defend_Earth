A classic arcade-style space shooter built with **HTML5 Canvas** and **Vanilla JavaScript**. Protect Earth from alien invasions, defeat powerful Bosses, and upgrade your arsenal!

* **Boss Fights:** Face a massive Boss with specialized Minions every 5 levels.
* **Power-ups:** * **P (Power):** Upgrades your weapon (Single -> Double -> Triple -> Laser).

* **High Scores:** Local leaderboard system to track the top 5 scores.
* **Visual Effects:** Dynamic particle explosions when the player or enemies are hit.
